Display Comparison

The Realme C83 5G and Realme 15x 5G both have a 6.8-inch HD+ display with a 144Hz refresh rate. However, the Realme 15x 5G offers up to 1200 nits brightness and Corning+ LCD protection, while the C83 5G reaches 900 nits. For a brighter display, the Realme 15x 5G is the better choice.

Camera Comparison

The Realme 15x 5G has a better camera setup with a 50MP Sony IMX852 rear camera and a 50MP AI front camera. The Realme C83 5G comes with a 13MP rear camera and a 5MP selfie camera. For photography and selfies, the Realme 15x 5G is the better choice.

Battery Comparison

Both smartphones come with a large 7000mAh battery, so battery backup is almost the same. Both also support reverse charging. If you want long battery life, both phones perform equally well.

Charging Comparison

The Realme C83 5G supports 15W fast charging, while the Realme 15x 5G supports much faster 60W SUPERVOOC charging. If fast charging is important, the Realme 15x 5G is the clear winner.

Overall Comparison

Both smartphones offer a large 7000mAh battery and a 144Hz display. The Realme C83 5G provides better benchmark performance and includes a VC Cooling System. The Realme 15x 5G offers much faster 60W charging, better cameras, more RAM and storage options, and smoother gaming. For most users, the Realme 15x 5G is the better overall choice.

Gaming Comparison

The Realme 15x 5G supports up to 60FPS BGMI, while the Realme C83 5G supports 40FPS BGMI. Although the C83 5G has a VC Cooling System, the Realme 15x 5G delivers smoother gameplay. For gaming, the Realme 15x 5G is the better option.

Performance Comparison

Both phones use the MediaTek Dimensity 6300 processor. However, the Realme C83 5G has a higher AnTuTu score (560,000+) and a 5334mm² VC Cooling System. The Realme 15x 5G offers more RAM and storage options. Overall, the Realme C83 5G has slightly better performance.
