Display Comparison

The Realme P4 Pro 5G and Realme P4 Power 5G both come with a 6.8-inch AMOLED display, 1.5K resolution, 144Hz refresh rate, and 6500 nits peak brightness. Both phones also support 1.07 billion colors and have Corning Gorilla Glass 7i protection. The P4 Pro 5G offers a 4D Curved AMOLED display and higher touch response, while the P4 Power 5G provides stronger IP66 + IP68 + IP69 protection. Overall, the display experience is slightly better on the Realme P4 Pro 5G.

Camera Comparison

The Realme P4 Pro 5G features a 50MP Sony IMX896 OIS main camera with an 8MP ultra-wide camera, while the Realme P4 Power 5G has a 50MP + 8MP ultra-wide camera with OIS support. The P4 Pro 5G offers a better front camera with a 50MP selfie camera, compared to the 16MP selfie camera on P4 Power. Overall, the Realme P4 Pro 5G is better for photography and selfies.

Battery Comparison

The Realme P4 Power 5G has a much larger 10000mAh battery, while the Realme P4 Pro 5G comes with a 7000mAh battery. Both phones support 80W Ultra Charge and reverse charging. If you want maximum battery backup for long usage, the Realme P4 Power 5G is the clear winner.

Charging Comparison

Both smartphones support 80W Ultra Charge technology, which provides fast charging. There is no major difference in charging speed between both phones. Both devices also come with a USB Type-C port and support reverse charging.

Gaming Comparison

Both phones offer strong gaming performance with a 7000mm² VC Cooling System. The Realme P4 Pro 5G supports up to 144FPS BGMI, while the Realme P4 Power 5G supports 90FPS BGMI. Due to higher FPS support and Snapdragon 7 Gen 4 chipset, the Realme P4 Pro 5G is better for gaming.

Overall Comparison

Both smartphones are powerful devices with AMOLED displays, fast charging, and strong performance. The Realme P4 Pro 5G is better for users who want higher performance, better selfies, smoother gaming, and a lightweight design. The Realme P4 Power 5G is better for users who need a huge 10000mAh battery and premium IP69 protection. Overall, the Realme P4 Pro 5G is the better all-round smartphone.

Performance Comparison

The Realme P4 Pro 5G uses the Snapdragon 7 Gen 4 processor, while the Realme P4 Power 5G uses the MediaTek Dimensity 7400 Ultra processor. Both phones have LPDDR4X RAM, UFS 3.1 storage, and 7000mm² VC Cooling System. The P4 Pro 5G achieves a higher AnTuTu score of 1.11M+, making it slightly better in performance.
