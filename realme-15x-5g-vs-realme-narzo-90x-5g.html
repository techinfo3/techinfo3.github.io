Display Comparison

The Realme 15x 5G comes with a 6.72-inch FHD+ LCD display with a 144Hz refresh rate, while the Realme Narzo 90x 5G features a 17.20cm OLED display with a 120Hz refresh rate. The NARZO 80 Pro offers better colors, 1.07 billion colors support, and higher peak brightness up to 4500 nits. However, the P4x 5G provides a higher 144Hz refresh rate for smoother scrolling and gaming. Overall, NARZO 80 Pro 5G is better for display quality, while P4x 5G is better for smoothness.

Camera Comparison

The Realme Narzo 90x 5G offers a better camera setup with a 50MP Sony IMX882 OIS camera and a 16MP selfie camera. The Realme 15x 5G has a 50MP main camera with a 2MP monochrome sensor and an 8MP front camera. NARZO 80 Pro provides better photography, video stability, and selfie quality due to OIS and higher resolution front camera.

Battery Comparison

The Realme 15x 5G has a bigger 7000mAh battery, while the Realme Narzo 90x 5G comes with a 6000mAh battery. The P4x 5G provides longer battery backup for daily use, gaming, and entertainment. If you want better battery life, Realme 15x 5G is the clear winner.

Charging Comparison

The Realme Narzo 90x 5G supports faster 80W SUPERVOOC charging, while the Realme 15x 5G supports 45W SuperVOOC charging. Both phones support USB Type-C and reverse charging, but NARZO 80 Pro 5G charges much faster.

Overall Comparison

The Realme 15x 5G is a great choice for users who want a large 7000mAh battery, 144Hz display, expandable storage, and good performance at a lower price. The Realme Narzo 90x 5G offers a better OLED display, faster charging, superior camera, stronger cooling, higher performance, and premium IP66+IP68+IP69 protection. Overall, Realme Narzo 90x 5G is the better choice for users who want a complete premium experience, while P4x 5G is better for battery-focused users.

Gaming Comparison

Both phones support 90FPS BGMI gaming. The Realme 15x 5G has a 144Hz refresh rate display, while the NARZO 80 Pro 5G offers a stronger cooling system and higher performance. For long gaming sessions, NARZO 80 Pro 5G has a slight advantage because of better cooling and higher AnTuTu performance.

Performance Comparison

Both smartphones use 4nm MediaTek Dimensity processors with ARM Mali-G615 GPU and UFS 3.1 storage. The Realme Narzo 90x 5G achieves a higher AnTuTu score of around 1,000,000, while the P4x 5G scores 780,000+. NARZO 80 Pro also has a larger 6050mm² VC Cooling System compared to 5300mm² on P4x 5G. Therefore, NARZO 80 Pro 5G offers better overall performance.
