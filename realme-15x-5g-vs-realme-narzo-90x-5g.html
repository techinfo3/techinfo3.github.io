Display Comparison

Both phones Realme 15x 5G and Realme Narzo 90x 5G feature a 6.8-inch LCD display with a 144Hz refresh rate, HD+ resolution, and 1200 nits HBM brightness. However, the Realme 15x 5G offers better durability with Corning protection and a much stronger IP69 rating. For display and protection, Realme 15x 5G is the better choice

Camera Comparison

The Realme 15x 5G offers a better camera experience with a 50MP Sony IMX852 rear camera and a 50MP front camera. The Realme Narzo 90x 5G has the same 50MP rear camera but only an 8MP selfie camera. For photography and selfies, Realme 15x 5G is the better choice.

Battery Comparison

Both smartphones Realme 15x 5G and Realme Narzo 90x 5G come with a 7000mAh battery, so battery backup is almost the same. They also support reverse charging and USB Type-C. In terms of battery life, both phones perform equally well.

Charging Comparison

The Realme 15x 5G and Realme Narzo 90x 5G both support 60W fast charging, so charging speed is almost the same. There is no major difference in charging performance.

Overall Comparison

Both smartphones Realme 15x 5G and Realme Narzo 90x 5G have the same Dimensity 6300 processor, 7000mAh battery, 144Hz display, and 60W fast charging. The Realme 15x 5G stands out with a 50MP selfie camera, IP69 protection, better speaker, and dual-mic noise cancellation. The Realme Narzo 90x 5G offers better gaming, a higher AnTuTu score, and a VC Cooling System. If you want better cameras and durability, choose Realme 15x 5G. If you want better gaming and performance, choose Realme Narzo 90x 5G.

Gaming Comparison

The Realme Narzo 90x 5G supports 90FPS BGMI and includes a 5300mm² VC Cooling System. The Realme 15x 5G supports only 60FPS BGMI, and its cooling system is not mentioned. For gaming, Realme Narzo 90x 5G is the better choice.

Performance Comparison

Both phones Realme 15x 5G and Realme Narzo 90x 5G use the MediaTek Dimensity 6300 processor. However, the Realme Narzo 90x 5G has a higher AnTuTu score (560K+), a 5300mm² VC Cooling System, and better optimization. For overall performance, Realme Narzo 90x 5G is slightly better.
