Display Comparison

The Realme P4x 5G offers a better display than the Realme Narzo 100 Lite 5G. It features a 6.72-inch FHD+ LCD with 144Hz refresh rate, 391 PPI, and 1000 nits peak brightness. The Narzo 100 Lite 5G has an HD+ LCD with 256 PPI and 900 nits brightness. Both support 144Hz refresh rate, but the P4x 5G provides a sharper and brighter viewing experience.

Camera Comparison

The Realme P4x 5G has a better camera setup with a 50MP + 2MP rear camera and an 8MP front camera. It also supports 4K video recording. The Narzo 100 Lite 5G offers a 13MP rear camera and 5MP selfie camera, making it suitable only for basic photography. Overall, the Realme P4x 5G delivers much better camera performance.

Battery Comparison

Both smartphones come with a 7000mAh battery and support reverse charging, so battery backup is almost the same. For daily use, both can easily last a full day or more on a single charge.

Gaming Comparison

The Realme P4x 5G supports 90FPS BGMI, while the Narzo 100 Lite 5G supports 60FPS BGMI. Both phones feature a 5300mm² VC Cooling System, but the stronger processor and higher FPS support make the Realme P4x 5G a much better choice for gaming.

Charging Comparison

The Realme P4x 5G supports 45W SUPERVOOC charging, while the Realme Narzo 100 Lite 5G supports only 15W charging. If you want much faster charging, the Realme P4x 5G is the clear winner.

Overall Comparison

Both phones offer a 7000mAh battery, but the Realme P4x 5G stands out with its Dimensity 7400 Ultra processor, 45W fast charging, better display, superior cameras, and 90FPS gaming support. The Realme Narzo 100 Lite 5G is a good option for users looking for a more affordable smartphone for basic daily use. If you want the best overall experience, Realme P4x 5G is the better choice.

Performance Comparison

The Realme P4x 5G is powered by the MediaTek Dimensity 7400 Ultra chipset with an AnTuTu score of around 780,000+. The Narzo 100 Lite 5G uses the Dimensity 6300 chipset with an AnTuTu score of around 560,000+. For multitasking, app performance, and daily speed, the Realme P4x 5G performs significantly better.
