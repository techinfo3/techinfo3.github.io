Display Comparison

The Realme P4 5G offers a smoother display with a 144Hz HyperGlow AMOLED panel, while the Realme NARZO 80 Pro 5G comes with a 120Hz OLED HyperGlow display. Both phones reach 4500 nits peak brightness and support 1.07 billion colors, but the P4 5G provides a higher refresh rate for smoother scrolling and gaming. If display smoothness is your priority, the Realme P4 5G is the better choice.

Camera Comparison

The Realme P4 5G features a 50MP + 8MP ultra-wide camera with a 16MP front camera, making it more versatile for landscape and group photography. The Realme NARZO 80 Pro 5G has a 50MP Sony IMX882 OIS camera, which offers better image stabilization, but its secondary camera is only a monochrome sensor. Overall, Realme P4 5G is better for all-round photography, while the NARZO 80 Pro is better for stable photos and videos.

Gaming Comparison

The Realme P4 5G offers a smoother display with a 144Hz HyperGlow AMOLED panel, while the Realme NARZO 80 Pro 5G comes with a 120Hz OLED HyperGlow display. Both phones reach 4500 nits peak brightness and support 1.07 billion colors, but the P4 5G provides a higher refresh rate for smoother scrolling and gaming. If display smoothness is your priority, the Realme P4 5G is the better choice.

Battery Comparison

The Realme P4 5G has a larger 7000mAh battery, whereas the Realme NARZO 80 Pro 5G packs a 6000mAh battery. Both phones support 80W fast charging and reverse charging. If you want longer battery backup, the Realme P4 5G is the winner.

Charging Comparison

Both smartphones support 80W fast charging, so charging speeds are almost the same. Since both include USB Type-C and 80W adapters, there is no major difference in charging performance.

Overall Comparison

Both smartphones offer excellent value in this price range. The Realme P4 5G stands out with its 144Hz AMOLED display, larger 7000mAh battery, better cooling system, and ultra-wide camera, making it the better choice for most users. The Realme NARZO 80 Pro 5G offers a lighter design, higher RAM options, Sony OIS camera, and stronger IP66/IP68/IP69 protection. If you want the best overall experience, Realme P4 5G is the better buy.

Performance Comparison

Both phones use a MediaTek Dimensity 7400 series chipset, LPDDR4X RAM, and UFS 3.1 storage. However, the Realme P4 5G has a slightly higher AnTuTu score (1.019M+) and a larger 7000mm² VC Cooling System, giving it a small advantage during heavy use. For overall performance, Realme P4 5G is slightly better.
