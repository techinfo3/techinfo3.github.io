Display Comparison

The Realme P4 5G has a much better display than the Realme P4x 5G. It features a 6.77-inch HyperGlow AMOLED panel with richer colors, deeper blacks, and better contrast. It also reaches 4500 nits peak brightness, making it easier to use outdoors. The phone is protected by Corning Gorilla Glass 7i, while the P4x 5G does not officially mention any display protection. Although both phones support a 144Hz refresh rate, the AMOLED display gives the Realme P4 5G a noticeably better viewing experience.

Camera Comparison

The Realme P4 5G offers the better camera setup. It has a 50MP main camera with an 8MP ultra-wide camera, allowing users to capture wider landscapes and group photos. The Realme P4x 5G replaces the ultra-wide camera with a 2MP monochrome sensor, which is less useful in everyday photography. The P4 5G also has a 16MP front camera, compared to the 8MP selfie camera on the P4x 5G. Overall, the Realme P4 5G provides better selfies, wider shots, and a more versatile camera experience.

Battery Comparison

Both the Realme P4 5G and Realme P4x 5G come with a 7000mAh Titan Battery, so battery backup is very similar. Both also support reverse charging, allowing you to charge other devices. For normal daily use, gaming, and video streaming, both smartphones can easily last an entire day. In terms of battery capacity, neither phone has an advantage.

Charging Comparison

The Realme P4 5G clearly wins in charging speed. It supports 80W Ultra Charge, while the Realme P4x 5G supports 45W Ultra Charge. This means the P4 5G can recharge its large 7000mAh battery much faster, making it the better option for users who don't like waiting long for their phone to charge.

Gaming Comparison

For gaming, both phones support 90FPS BGMI, so gameplay is smooth on both devices. However, the Realme P4 5G has a larger cooling system, which keeps the phone cooler during extended gaming sessions. If you play games for long periods, the P4 5G offers a more stable gaming experience with better heat management.

Overall Comparison

Both smartphones share the same processor, battery capacity, RAM type, storage, and 90FPS gaming support. However, the Realme P4 5G stands out with its AMOLED display, 80W fast charging, better camera system, stronger cooling system, higher peak brightness, and Gorilla Glass 7i protection. The Realme P4x 5G is still a good option for users looking to save a little money, but if your budget allows, the Realme P4 5G delivers a more complete flagship-like experience and is the better overall choice.

Performance Comparison

Both smartphones use the MediaTek Dimensity 7400 Ultra 5G processor with the Mali-G615 GPU, so everyday performance is very similar. However, the Realme P4 5G has a slight advantage thanks to its higher AnTuTu score (1,019,000+) and a larger 7000mm² VC Cooling System, compared to the 5300mm² VC Cooling in the P4x 5G. This helps the P4 5G maintain better performance during long gaming sessions and heavy multitasking.
