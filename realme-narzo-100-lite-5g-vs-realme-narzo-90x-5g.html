Display Comparison

Both phones come with a 6.8-inch FHD+ LCD display and a 144Hz refresh rate, so scrolling and gaming feel equally smooth. However, the Realme Narzo 90x 5G has higher brightness (up to 1200 nits HBM) and an IP65 rating, making it slightly better for outdoor use.

Camera Comparison

The Realme Narzo 90x 5G has a 50MP Sony IMX852 AI camera with an 8MP front camera, giving better photo quality and selfies. The Narzo 100 Lite 5G offers a 13MP AI rear camera and a 5MP selfie camera, which are good for basic photography.

Battery Comparison

Both smartphones pack a 7000mAh battery with reverse charging support, so battery backup is almost the same. You can easily get a full day or more of normal usage on either phone.

Charging Comparison

Both phones come with a 6.8-inch FHD+ LCD display and a 144Hz refresh rate, so scrolling and gaming feel equally smooth. However, the Realme Narzo 90x 5G has higher brightness (up to 1200 nits HBM) and an IP65 rating, making it slightly better for outdoor use.

Gaming Comparison

The Realme Narzo 90x 5G supports 60W fast charging, while the Realme Narzo 100 Lite 5G supports only 15W fast charging. If fast charging is important, the Narzo 90x 5G is the clear winner.

Overall Comparison

Both smartphones offer a 7000mAh battery, 144Hz display, and the Dimensity 6300 processor. However, the Realme Narzo 90x 5G stands out with 60W fast charging, a better 50MP Sony camera, higher display brightness, 90FPS BGMI support, and IP65 protection. If your budget allows, the Realme Narzo 90x 5G is the better overall choice, while the Realme Narzo 100 Lite 5G is a good option for users looking to save money.

Performance Comparison

Both phones use the MediaTek Dimensity 6300 processor with LPDDR4X RAM and have an AnTuTu score of around 560K. Performance is almost the same for daily use, but the Narzo 90x 5G offers up to 18GB Dynamic RAM, giving it a small advantage in multitasking.
